Dicey  

Game #1

In this game, you roll one die, and you can either add the number shown on the die to your score, or subtract it from your score. Try to get a score of exactly 10. Try it out now:

  • Click Start in the upper right.
  • Click Roll dice and either Add or Subtract the value, until you get 10.

When you have played a few times, and have a good idea of what to do on each turn, click Next above, and you can create a program to play the game for you.
