Dicey  

Game #5

In this game, you roll two dice, and again you can either add the sum of their values or subtract the (positive) difference between them. But, new rules:

  • You win by getting a score of 21 or higher.
  • You lose if you get a score between 13 and 20. So, you have to look to get 9, 10, 11, or 12, and then get a big roll.
  • If you roll doubles, the 13-20 rule is suspended, and you can freely add up until you get to 21. But, if you roll doubles again, and your score is above 12, you go back to 0.
